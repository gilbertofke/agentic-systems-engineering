# Agentic Systems Engineering

This repository documents the design, development, and deployment of agentic AI systems — from foundational concepts like retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) to orchestrated multi-agent architectures integrated into real-world business workflows.

The focus is on engineering systems that connect language models to data, processes, and decision-making across domains such as sales, finance, and operations.

## Structure

* Foundations → Core primitives (embeddings, retrieval, vector search)
* Abstractions → Frameworks (LangChain)
* Orchestration → Multi-agent systems (LangGraph)
* Production → APIs, deployment, scaling
* Business Systems → Real-world implementations

## Goal

To develop deep, practical expertise in designing and building AI systems that operate reliably in production environments.
